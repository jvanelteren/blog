In 2024 I tried the rowing machine also known as the ergometer. Basically rowing indoors without ever moving a bit. Because it’s a good body workout and less prone to injury.

So this got a little out of control, in a good way. In total I managed to row 1000km (or 1 million meters).

In April I started doing longer distances at a slower pace. End of September I made the visualisation above for the first time and came up with the idea to reach 1 million meters in a year.

So that was tough but it was reached! Including a marathon December 14th and 100km in a week.

Here you see the split times. The small dots represent the short rows, which are of course faster than a longer training.

At the end of the year I was steadily going slower. Of course I was also rowing longer, but maybe got a bit fatigued.

Anyway, it was fun!
